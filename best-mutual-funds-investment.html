Mutual Funds: A Simple Way to Build Wealth

  • Mutual Funds in India are professionally managed and they diversify the Investments
  • Ideally suitable to investors who do not have the time & expertise in identifying the right stock.
  • Mutual fund investments when used right can lead to good returns, keeping risk at a minimum.
  • Especially great for people who are not experts in stock market dynamics as these are run by experienced fund managers.
  • Balance potential returns with a risk-adjusted strategy for long-term growth.

Explore the Top Mutual Funds to Invest

Equity Funds

Predominantly invests in Stocks with an aim to provide long term capital appreciation.

Hybrid Funds

Invests in Equity Stocks & Fixed Income Securities with an aim to protect capital and generate capital appreciation.

Index Funds

Invests in Equity Stocks & These are all passive funds replicates the Index it follows.

Debt Funds

Predominantly invests in fixed income securities with an aim to protect the capital with a decent return.

ETF

An ETF is an Exchange Traded Fund, trades like a common stock on a stock exchange.

NFO

A New Fund Offer (NFO) is the initial sale of units in a mutual fund scheme at a face value, typically ₹10. It enables investors to participate in a new investment opportunity launched by the fund house.

Spotlight on the Best Tax-Saving Mutual Funds (ELSS)

Equity Linked Savings Schemes (ELSS) not only provide tax benefits under Section 80C of the Income Tax Act but also offer the potential for attractive returns. By investing in ELSS mutual funds, investors can optimize tax savings while participating in the growth potential of equities, making them a preferred choice for tax-efficient wealth creation.
