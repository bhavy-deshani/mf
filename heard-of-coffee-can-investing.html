Have You Heard of Coffee Can Investing?

If you are a lazy investor and do not have patience to look through your equity portfolio often, then “Coffee Can investing” is for you! Essentially, coffee can investing is a low-risk way to build enormous wealth. In short, you purchase shares of outstanding companies and hold on to them for a long period of time, say 10 years, without actively buying or selling them. Essentially, you are putting your money in a “coffee can” and forgetting about it while trusting that these strong companies will grow over time. Coffee can investing is for people who do not have the time or expertise to monitor their investment portfolio closely. It is a “buy and forget” approach to investing in shares.

Story behind the coffee can

In old west America, people used coffee cans to store many things. They used to store valuables and important things in them and hide them under a mattress or someplace where no one could find them. This system was prevalent before the banking system was formed.

The old story behind the term goes thus: In 1960, there was a fund manager called Robert Kirby. He had a client whose husband bought shares of $5000 each of the company Xerox on his recommendation but never sold those shares. After he died, Kirby discovered that by holding on to the shares, he had created enormous wealth. His investments were worth more than $8,00,000 at the current date. This led Kirby to coin the term “Coffee Can investing”.

In India, Sourabh Mukherjee wrote a book titled “Coffee Can Investing” and introduced the term and made it popular in India. The book was co-authored with Rakshit Rajan and Pranab Uniyal. On a lighter note, Indian mothers have been storing money in grocery boxes or jars/ vessels and using them later to buy things or in investments.

Although the concept sounds simple, it is very difficult to implement in reality. It is extremely difficult to see the volatility in the market and still hold on to stocks for over 10 years.

Coffee can investing criteria

Ideally for coffee can investing, you must choose fundamentally strong companies with an excellent competitive edge in the market. When building a coffee can portfolio, you look for stock quality, which signifies a strong company. There are some specifics that could guide you to choose a stock for coffee can investing:

• The company should have been in existence for at least 10 years

• The market capitalization of the company must be over a 100 crores

• The company must have shown a minimum of 10% year-on-year profit growth

• It must be a business with a high ROCE (Return of Capital Employed) of at least 15% in the last 10 years

• The company must have a strong focus on sustainable competitive advantages and create a strong brand for itself.

Key principles of coffee can investing

1. Quality over quantity

This is the first rule of coffee can investing. Focus on high-quality companies that have good track records, financials and competitive advantages. You will need to do extensive research and select the right stocks for your coffee can portfolio.

2. Long-term perspective

This type of investment is not for people who are looking for quick gains. You will have to hold on to your stock portfolio for an extended period of time. With this approach, any short-term market fluctuations will not affect you. Additionally, you benefit from the power of compounding. You cannot be impulsive. You must have patience and discipline.

3. Minimal trading

Once you have selected your stocks, you must buy and forget them. This will help you minimize the short-term noise in the market. It will also minimize transaction costs.

4. Diversification

The size of your coffee can portfolio need not be very large (It can be about 10 to 15). However, ensure that you spread your investments across different sectors. This will prevent any concentration risk and capture growth opportunities from various industries.

Implementing coffee can investing

To begin implementing your coffee can strategy, follow a few steps:

1. Research and stock selection

Identify companies with a good and consistent record of growth and profitability. Go over the coffee can investing criteria and ensure that you tick all the boxes.

2. Build your portfolio

Once you have identified the potential companies that you want to invest in, build a diverse portfolio of 10-15 stocks from businesses across various sectors. Choose from IT services, consumer goods, healthcare and finance.

3. Invest and hold

Hold on to your chosen stocks for a long period of say 10 years. Resist the need to make changes to your portfolio based on market dynamics which are very short-term in nature.

4. Regular review

Although the principle of coffee can investing is to “buy and forget”, you could review your portfolio annually. This will be required to ensure that the companies continue to meet your criteria. Do not make frequent changes and trade stocks unless there is a fundamental shift the prospects of the company.

5. Reinvest dividends

When you chosen stocks pay dividends, just reinvest them. This will help you with the power of compounding over time.

Challenges of coffee can investing

Before you begin with your coffee can investment strategy, it is important to be aware of the challenges in the method:

• Difficulty in stock selection: The key to identify the right stocks for coffee can investing. This requires a lot of research and analysis. An individual investor will find it challenging to find a company that ticks all the criteria.

• Lack of flexibility: Since the strategy is to “buy and hold”, there are many short-term market movements that present opportunities which you may miss. You could also cut losses if the fundamentals of the company you chose deteriorates.

• Concentration risk: If you have a very small portfolio, the potential of poor performance from one or two companies could affect your overall returns.

• Changing market dynamics: Today’s fast-paced business environment can present new challenges to companies. Various things could cause disruptions. It is important to have confidence that your chosen companies can adapt and thrive over the long-term.

Conclusion

Coffee can investing is a powerful, long-term approach to building wealth. You must focus on high-quality companies with a good potential. This approach also requires patience and discipline. It is an attractive strategy for those seeking a simple and practical approach to building wealth in the long-term. Before jumping into it, it is important to consider your financial goals, risk tolerance and the investment horizon. Like everything else, the approach has its own challenges and it is important to be aware of them.
